PS_WRITER User Manual

Welcome to the PS_WRITER User Manual. This guide will help you understand and use the platform effectively.

Getting Started

To start using PS_WRITER, you need to create an account and log in. Once logged in, you can access the dashboard and other features.

Bot Editor

The Bot Editor allows you to create and customize trading bots. You can define your own strategies and automate your trading process.

Strategy

The Strategy section provides access to various predefined trading strategies. You can select and implement these strategies in your trading bots.

Chart

The Chart feature enables you to analyze market trends and make informed trading decisions. You can view real-time charts and use various technical indicators.

Trading Strategies

Even Strategy & Entry
Criteria When numbers (2, 4, 6, 8) indicate 11.5% and above, place an Even contract. Apply to all volatilities from Volatility 10 to Volatility 100.
Main Volatility Volatility 100 index
Entry Point Wait until cursor hits odd numbers (1, 3, 5, 7, 9) at least 4–5 times. Then run bot when an even number is hit.
Odd Strategy & Entry
Criteria When numbers (1, 3, 5, 7, 9) indicate 11% and above, place an Odd contract.
Main Volatility Volatility 75 index
Entry Point Wait until cursor hits even numbers (0, 2, 4, 6, 8) 3–4 times. Then run bot when an odd number is hit.
Over 0 & 1 Strategy
Criteria Use 1 tick. Green bar ≥ 10.5%. Red bar ≤ 9%.
Entry Point Cursor should hit 0 or 1 once or twice before entry.
Over 2 Strategy
Criteria Any digit among 3–9 should show 10% or higher.
Entry Point After an "Under 2" trade, wait for cursor to hit 0–2, then trade Over.
Digit Differs Strategy
Criteria Use 1 tick. Predict digit with < 9%. Green bar should be below 12%.
Entry Point Digit should appear at least once before entry.
Rise Strategy
Criteria Use trendlines, moving averages, and volume to detect uptrend.
Entry Timing Enter on pullback near support.
Risk Management Stop-loss just below support level.
Fall Strategy
Criteria Use trendlines and moving averages to detect downtrend.
Entry Timing Enter on bounce near resistance.
Risk Management Stop-loss above resistance level.
